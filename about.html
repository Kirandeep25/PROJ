ABOUT MANGAL SINGH AND SONS

We have been in the timber and woodworks business since 1950. It all started from a sawmill and then got expanded to rendering carpentry services. We are widely renowned for the high quality timber and transport it to many places all over India, especially Delhi, Haryana, Himachal. Our timber acts as a raw material to fulfill needs of the cuistomer parties. Never have we received any complaints about the supposed quality of our lumber supplied, it has always been upto the mark, according to the customer requirements. So, if you are a party that requires lumber as a raw product, you can contact us or visit us. For our contact details, you can navigate to the contact details section of the website, where you can find our active cell phone numbers, e-mail ID. To quality assure and check the product before buying, you can certainly visit our sawmill at Jail Road, Gurdaspur 143521, infront of Singh Sabha Gurudwara. As the products section of our page already highlights, we manufacture high quality goods that can beautify and furnish your homes greatly. We specifically deal in Door Frames, Window Frames, Wardrobes, Kitchen Cabinets, Other types of indoor and outdoor furniture.
